Code of Conduct

Server Rules

All players must follow these core rules at all times to keep the RP fair and immersive.

01

No Deathmatch (DM)

Killing without a valid in-character reason is forbidden. All conflict must be story-driven.

02

No Metagaming

Using out-of-character information to influence in-character decisions is not allowed.

03

No Powergaming

Do not force actions on other players or perform unrealistic feats for unfair advantages.

04

Respect All Players

Harassment, racism, and hate speech are permanently bannable offenses.

05

No Hacking / Cheating

Any form of cheating or exploiting bugs will result in a permanent ban.

06

Stay In Character

Always maintain your character while in-game. Use /ooc for out-of-character chat.
