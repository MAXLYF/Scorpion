Code of Conduct

Server Rules

Everything you need to know to play fair and have the best RP experience.

Category 01

General Rules

01

Respect & Toxicity

Harassment, OOC toxicity, and hate speech are strictly prohibited. Respect all community members.

02

No Hacking/Exploiting

Any use of third-party programs or exploiting game bugs for an advantage will result in a permanent ban.

Category 02

Roleplay Standards

03

Deathmatch (DM)

Killing or attacking players without a valid IC (In-Character) reason is not allowed.

04

Metagaming

Using OOC (Out-Of-Character) info in-game is forbidden. Your character only knows what they've learned IC.

05

Powergaming

Forcing actions on others or performing unrealistic feats is prohibited. Keep it grounded.

06

Value Your Life

You must act realistically when your life is threatened. Do not perform suicidal actions.

Category 03

Chat & Voice

07

OOC in IC Chat

Keep OOC talk out of IC channels. Use /b for OOC chat and keep it to a minimum.

08

Mic Quality

A working, clear microphone is required for voice RP. Avoid loud background noise.
